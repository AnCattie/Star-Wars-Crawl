The BeCode story

By An Cattie & Quinn Loots

It all started on the 17th of October. We were selected to joing the journey of BeCode Genk - Hopper.

On the first day we assembled our workplace with brand new chairs and got to know eachother by personal question games.

On the second day we've installed the operating systems of our laptops and personalized it with a BeCode-sticker.

We've learned a lot of new skills, working with different coding-programs.

Also we've learned a new coding-language with "GIT".

We've also learned how to network on Linkedin, Ryver, etc.

We also work all as a team and no one gets behind.

each day we keep learning each day.
